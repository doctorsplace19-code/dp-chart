For DOT Tests — Required Info

Federal (DOT) urine tests require a few extra details at ordering. We'll prompt you for each — it takes seconds.

  • ✓DER — your Designated Employer Representative (name, phone & email)
  • ✓DOT Agency — FMCSA, FAA, FRA, FTA, PHMSA, or USCG
  • ✓Reason for Test — pre-employment, random, post-accident, reasonable suspicion, or return-to-duty
Non-DOT tests don't require DER or agency info — just order and go.
