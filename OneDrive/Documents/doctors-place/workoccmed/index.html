What We Do

Full-Service Occupational Health

Everything your workforce needs — DOT compliance, pre-employment, OSHA screenings, and employer health programs managed in one portal.

🚛

DOT Physical Exams

FMCSA-certified medical examinations for CDL drivers. Covers all DOT agencies — FMCSA, FAA, FRA, FTA, PHMSA, USCG. Medical Examiner Certificate issued same day.

$110 flat rate
🩺

Pre-Employment Physicals

Fitness-for-duty evaluations for new hires in any industry — construction, healthcare, manufacturing, staffing, and more. Musculoskeletal and cardiovascular assessment included.

Non-DOT · All industries
🧪

Drug & Alcohol Testing

DOT-compliant and non-DOT urine drug screens, oral fluid, hair follicle, and evidential breath alcohol testing (EBT). Results sent directly to your MRO and employer portal.

5, 10 & expanded panels
🔁

Random Testing Program (C-TPA)

FMCSA-compliant consortium enrollment and random pool management. Quarterly draws (50% drug / 10% alcohol), MRO review, Clearinghouse reporting, and audit-ready recordkeeping.

From $49/yr per driver
😷

Respirator Fit Testing

OSHA-compliant qualitative (QLFT) and quantitative (QNFT) respirator fit testing. Required for workers using tight-fitting respirators under 29 CFR 1910.134.

OSHA 29 CFR 1910.134
📝

OSHA Medical Questionnaire

Physician-reviewed OSHA respiratory medical evaluation questionnaire (Appendix C). Required before any employee uses a tight-fitting respirator in the workplace.

Physician reviewed
🦻

Audiogram Testing

OSHA-compliant baseline and annual audiometric testing for workers exposed to occupational noise. Required under OSHA's Hearing Conservation Standard (29 CFR 1910.95).

OSHA 29 CFR 1910.95
🫁

Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)

Spirometry testing for workers exposed to respiratory hazards. Required for employees in industries with silica, asbestos, or chemical exposure under OSHA standards.

Spirometry · OSHA compliant
👁️

Vision & Ishihara Color Test

Snellen visual acuity and Ishihara color plate testing for pre-employment and annual occupational health screenings. Required for many safety-sensitive roles.

Pre-employment · Annual
📋

Return-to-Work / Fit for Duty

Post-injury and post-illness fitness-for-duty evaluations to clear employees for return to work. Documentation provided for HR and workers' compensation records.

Workers' comp · HR clearance
🧬

Clearinghouse Query & Reporting

FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse pre-employment queries, annual queries, and violation reporting. Required for all DOT-regulated employers with CDL drivers.

FMCSA required
💉

Vaccinations & Titers

Occupational vaccination programs — flu, Hep A & B, Tdap, MMR, varicella, and more. Titer testing for immunity verification for healthcare workers and new hires.

Healthcare · Construction · Schools
