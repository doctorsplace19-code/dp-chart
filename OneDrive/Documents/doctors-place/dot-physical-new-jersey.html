Before You Arrive

What to Bring to Your DOT Physical

Having these items ready ensures your exam goes smoothly and your certificate is issued same day.

🪪

Photo ID / CDL

Government-issued photo ID and your commercial driver's license or permit.

💊

Medication List

All current prescriptions with dosages. Includes insulin, blood pressure medications, and sleep aids.

👓

Glasses or Contacts

Wear your corrective lenses if you use them. Vision is tested as part of the exam.

📋

Medical Records (if applicable)

Bring relevant records for diabetes, heart disease, sleep apnea, or high blood pressure if you have a history.

🩺

CPAP Data (if applicable)

If treated for sleep apnea, bring recent CPAP compliance data (last 90 days).

💳

Payment

Cash or card accepted. DOT physicals are not billed to insurance — payment at time of service.

The Process

In and Out Same Day

Most CDL drivers are done in 30–45 minutes, certificate in hand.

1

Walk In or Book Online

No appointment required Mon–Sat. Book online at portal.dot-physical.net to be seen faster.

2

Complete Medical History Form

MCSA-5875 form filled out at the clinic. Takes about 5–10 minutes.

3

Examination

Vision, hearing, blood pressure, urinalysis, and physical evaluation by our FMCSA-certified examiner.

4

Certificate Issued

Receive your Medical Examiner Certificate (MEC) and MCSA-5876 same day. Results submitted to the FMCSA National Registry.
